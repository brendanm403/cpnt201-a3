Tarantula Dealers

Tarantulas are animals that most people live in fear of. However, there exists a significant chunk of crazy people who keep them as pets. Here at Webby Bois we are proud members of this subset of crazies. While tarantulas are not interactive pets, they are quite fascinating to observe. Keeping tarantulas as pets has also been a method some people have used to get over arachnophobia.

Tarantulas are divided into two main categories- old world and new world. Old world tarantulas are typically more aggressive, faster, and have more potent venom. This subset of Tarantulas comes from the eastern hemisphere. Old worlds are not recommended as pets for those who are new to the hobby. New world tarantulas are more docile, have lower top speeds, and have less potent venom. These tarantulas originate from the western hemisphere and are the recommended choice for newer keepers.
